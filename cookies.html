🍪 Golden Cookies Charter Absolutely Zero Cookies

We serve 100% digital transparency: no crumbs, no trackers, no hidden storage. Your visit is ephemeral — like the scent of saffron.

📜 1. The Zero Digital Footprint Promise

Ylem Culinary Singularity does not use any HTTP cookies, web storage (localStorage, sessionStorage), IndexedDB, or any client-side persistent storage mechanisms. We never deploy tracking pixels, fingerprinting scripts, or advertising identifiers. Your browser communicates solely with our static HTML/CSS/JS files — no cookies are created, read, or transmitted at any point. This policy is absolute and verified across all devices and sessions.

🍪 2. Types of Cookies We DO NOT Use

❌ Session cookies (strictly necessary)
❌ Persistent cookies (expiration dates)
❌ Third-party tracking cookies (advertising)
❌ Analytics cookies (Google, Matomo, etc.)
❌ Flash cookies / Local Shared Objects
❌ Social media cookies (no widgets or share buttons)
❌ Authentication / preference cookies
❌ Cross-site tracking (no embedded frames)

Because we collect no user data nor session state, we never require cookie consent banners. Our website is fully compliant with the ePrivacy Directive, GDPR, CCPA, and other regulations without pop-ups or modals.

📦 3. No localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB

Modern browsers offer other storage mechanisms that some sites misuse for tracking. We completely refrain from using any of them. Open your browser’s developer tools (Application tab) while visiting Ylem Culinary Singularity — you will see zero entries under Local Storage, Session Storage, and IndexedDB. No cache abuse, no etag tracking, no supercookies. Your digital environment remains untouched.

🔍 Verification for the skeptical chef: Right-click → Inspect → Application → Storage. All sections empty. No cookies in “Cookies” list for this domain. This is our permanent state.

⚙️ 4. No external dependencies or tracking scripts

Our HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are fully self-hosted and monolithic. There are no external CDN calls, no Google Fonts, no analytics SDKs, no embedded YouTube videos, no advertising network requests. This guarantees that no third party can drop cookies or harvest metadata. Even icons are inline UTF-8 symbols or simple SVG shapes. Every request stays strictly within the ylem domain, and we do not set or read any headers related to tracking.

🌐 Network sandbox verification: Open Network tab in DevTools — no calls to external APIs, no beacon requests, no analytics endpoints. Pure static golden content.

🚫 5. No Cookie Banner — Explained

Because we do not use any non-exempt cookies or trackers, there is no legal requirement to display a cookie consent notice. EU ePrivacy Directive and GDPR only require consent for non-essential storage or access to information on user’s terminal equipment. Since our site performs zero such operations, we voluntarily forego intrusive banners. Your browsing experience is clean, faster, and distraction-free.

💡 Fun fact: Many websites claim no tracking but still embed external fonts. We do not. Every element is local and private.
