📜 Terms of Privacy

The Golden Transparency Manifesto — how we honor your digital essence, just as we honor saffron and turmeric. Zero data extraction. Pure culinary light.

⚖️ 1. The Primordial Pact

Ylem Culinary Singularity (“Ylem”, “we”, “our”, “the Singularity”) operates as a radical knowledge garden. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data through our static web experience. Our website contains no analytics scripts, no tracking pixels, no fingerprinting mechanisms, and no third-party data collectors. Your visit is ephemeral — like the bloom of a marigold — leaving no trace on our servers beyond the anonymous request to deliver HTML/CSS/JS files. In alignment with the golden ratio philosophy, we respect the integrity of your presence: what you read, taste conceptually, and explore remains yours alone.

🌿 Zero-Cookie Zone No authentication, no registration, no comments, no forms that store personal data. The only "interaction" is your browser requesting our pages, which is not logged in any user-identifiable manner. Our hosting infrastructure maintains standard server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, requested resources) purely for security and performance reasons; these logs are anonymized weekly and never shared with any third party, nor used to build profiles.

🔒 2. Information We Never Collect

Unlike commercial gastronomy platforms, Ylem does not pursue your data. We explicitly do not collect:

  • ❌ Names, email addresses, phone numbers or any contact identifiers (we provide a contact page with a static mailto link – no server-side capture).
  • ❌ Behavioral tracking (clicks, scrolling, time on page).
  • ❌ Cookies — we set zero persistent or session cookies except purely technical ones required by server infrastructure (none exist).
  • ❌ Device fingerprints, location data, or IP-derived geolocation.
  • ❌ Interaction with external social media widgets — there are none.

Our atelier contact (email, phone, physical address) is provided solely for pilgrims of flavor to reach us voluntarily — any correspondence handled offline is separate from web activity. We do not build mailing lists or CRM entries unless you explicitly ask to be added (and even then, we treat it with the same golden ethics).

🍪 3. Cookies & Local Storage — The Absence

Check our Cookies Policy for full detail, but in essence: this site uses no tracking cookies and never stores any information on your device. No “necessary” or “functional” cookies are deployed. We do not employ any localStorage or sessionStorage for persistent IDs, preferences, or analytics. Your browser remains pristine of Ylem fingerprints after you leave. This is our culinary singularity oath: you come, you feast on knowledge, you depart without a crumb of data left behind. Any external links (like to academic papers or open sourdough communities) are opened in new tabs, but those sites have their own policies — we encourage you to read them.

🤝 4. Third-Party Services & Integrations

Ylem Culinary Singularity uses no third-party scripts, CDNs that log personally identifiable information, or external fonts that gather analytics. All styling is inline or embedded; all assets are self-hosted. There are no advertisement networks, affiliate links, or tracking redirects. Our website is purely static HTML/CSS/JavaScript (vanilla) and does not rely on Google Fonts, jQuery CDN, or any external API. Even our console greeting is purely atmospheric. This guarantees that your browsing remains within the boundaries of our golden manifesto — no data leaks through the back door of a tracking pixel.

📡 5. Server Logs & Anonymization Protocol

Our web host (a privacy-oriented provider located within the EU) maintains standard access logs that include IP addresses, request timestamps, user agent strings, and the pages visited. However, these logs are:

  • • Retained for a maximum of 14 days for security diagnostics (e.g., defending against denial-of-service).
  • • Rotated and anonymized: after the retention window, IP addresses are hashed irreversibly and user-agent strings aggregated generically.
  • • Never sold, rented, or shared with any third party, including advertisers or data brokers.
  • • Used solely to ensure the integrity of the Ylem website and to analyze broad traffic volume (e.g., “250 visits today”), without ever identifying individuals.

We consider this minimal logging a technical necessity, aligned with the Golden Rule of digital ethics. No attempt is made to correlate logs across visits or track returning users — each visit stands alone, like an individual spice note in a golden broth.

📧 6. Contact & Voluntary Correspondence

On our Contact page we provide a physical address, phone number, and a mailto email link. If you reach out to us via email or phone, we will only use your contact details to respond to your inquiry. We do not add you to any newsletter or marketing list unless you explicitly request it. After a conversation concludes, any personal information shared (such as your email address) is either deleted or stored securely offline if needed for follow-up. You have full rights to request deletion of any information you’ve shared — simply email us with “Data Erasure Request” in the subject line. Our commitment: no hoarding, no commercial exploitation, only the honest exchange of culinary philosophy.

👶 7. Children’s Privacy & Golden Guardianship

Ylem Culinary Singularity does not knowingly collect any information from individuals under the age of 13. Our content is family-friendly (celebrating saffron risotto, turmeric halwa, and fermentation wisdom) and does not target children specifically. Since we gather no personal information anyway, there is no risk of inadvertent collection. If a parent or guardian believes we have inadvertently received data from a minor, please contact us and we will ensure prompt deletion. The Golden Ratio Kitchen welcomes curious minds of all ages but always under the supervision of a flavor guardian.

🌍 8. International Users & GDPR / CCPA Alignment

Because we collect no personal data and deploy no tracking, Ylem Culinary Singularity is already compliant with the strictest privacy frameworks, including the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not need “consent banners” or cookie pop-ups because there are no data processing activities to consent to. Your rights to access, rectification, erasure, and data portability are fully respected — though we possess zero data concerning you. For any specific inquiries, contact our Data Ethics Officer at privacy@ylemsingularity.chef. We will respond within 15 business days with a golden affirmation of our emptiness.

🛡️ 9. Security Measures

We take reasonable technical measures to protect our hosted files. The website is served over HTTPS (TLS 1.3) to encrypt data between your browser and our server, preventing interception. Regular security audits ensure that no malicious code is injected. As there are no user accounts or input forms, attack surfaces are minimal. You can verify our security headers — we implement strict Content-Security-Policy and X-Frame-Options to further harden the site. In the unlikely event of a security breach that might affect visitor anonymity, we will publicly disclose the incident via this website within 72 hours.

📝 10. Changes to These Terms of Privacy

Ylem Culinary Singularity may update this document occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The “last revised” date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. We will never retroactively alter our commitment to zero tracking. Any modifications will be announced via a small notice on the homepage for 30 days. Your continued exploration of the Ylem recipe singularity after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Terms of Privacy. However, since we never retain data about you, you are always free to browse without creating a user relationship.
