🔒 Golden Privacy Charter Zero Data Footprint

At Ylem Culinary Singularity, your digital solitude is as sacred as saffron. Read our unwavering pledge below.

📜 1. The Singularity Promise

We exist to share the yellow flame of gastronomy — not to harvest your data. This privacy policy codifies our commitment: zero tracking, zero persistent cookies, zero third-party analytics, and zero backend storage of personal information. Every recipe, every word of our 58,000+ culinary lexicon, is served without invisible surveillance. You are a guest, not a data point.

🍋 2. What We Do NOT Collect

✖️ IP addresses or geolocation
✖️ Browser fingerprints or device IDs
✖️ Cookies (session/persistent) — none!
✖️ Form submissions stored on servers
✖️ Email lists or sign-up databases
✖️ Analytics (no Google, no Meta)

The only "data" that exists is the text you voluntarily type into our golden contact form; that message is never transmitted to any database — it's shown locally as a simulation and vanishes on page refresh. No logging. No storage. No server-side capture.

✉️ 3. Your Culinary Inquiries & Email Communication

If you choose to reach out via our displayed email address (hello@ylemsingularity.chef) or phone number, those communications are handled exclusively through standard email or telephone protocols. We never automatically archive your information. Any conversation remains between you and our atelier team and is deleted after correspondence concludes unless you explicitly ask us to retain a collaboration thread. We do not build contact lists, CRM entries, or newsletters. Your message, your sovereignty.

📧 press@ylemsingularity.chef – for media only, no data mining

🍪 4. Absolutely No Cookies — Ever

This website does not set any HTTP cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, IndexedDB, or any browser-based tracking mechanism. No consent banner is required or displayed because nothing is stored on your device. We also do not use web beacons, pixel tags, or fingerprinting scripts. Your visit is ephemeral — like the aroma of fresh turmeric. You can verify via browser devtools: zero cookies, zero storage entries.

🌐 5. No Third-Party Services / External Calls

Our HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are entirely self-contained. There are no external fonts, no CDN trackers, no social media widgets that load from external servers. All icons and visual elements are inline or system-native. Your browser never makes hidden requests to analytics endpoints, ad networks, or data brokers. Even our map placeholder is a mock-up — no Google Maps API, no embedded scripts. Total isolation = total privacy.

🧒 6. Children’s Privacy & Golden Guidance

Our content is family-friendly, focused on recipes, cooking techniques, and flavor philosophy. We do not knowingly collect any information from persons under 13 years of age. Since we collect nothing whatsoever, no parental consent mechanism is required. Parents can safely explore Ylem Culinary Singularity with their children without risks of behavioral tracking.

🛡️ 7. Your Rights & Golden Sovereignty

Because we store zero personal data on our side, your rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability are automatically honored: there is nothing to delete or export. However, if you have any doubt or wish to verify our practices, you can contact us via the physical address listed in the footer. We gladly respond to any privacy inquiry within 72 hours.
